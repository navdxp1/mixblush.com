WHAT TYPE OF EYE MAKEUP YOU WANT?


EXPLORE EYE MAKEUP CATEGORIES

Smokey Eyes

"Smokey eyes is a bold and dramatic makeup look that emphasizes the eyes with deep, sultry shades of black, brown, or grey. The technique involves blending darker eyeshadow along the eyelids and into the crease, creating a smudged, smoky effect. It's a versatile style that can range from a soft, everyday glam to a more intense, evening-ready look. Whether paired with a nude lip for balance or a bold red lip for added drama, smokey eyes are a timeless way to make a statement."

Cut Crease

"A cut crease is a striking eye makeup technique that enhances the shape of the eyes by creating a sharp contrast between the eyelid and the crease. This look involves defining the crease with a darker eyeshadow while keeping the eyelid bright and clean, often using concealer or a light-colored eyeshadow to create a crisp, well-defined edge. The cut crease adds depth and dimension, making the eyes appear bigger and more lifted. Whether kept soft for a natural look or intensified with bold colors and glitter for a dramatic effect, this technique is a favorite for achieving a glamorous and polished eye look."

Halo Eyes

"Halo eyes is a mesmerizing makeup technique that creates a bright, spotlight effect in the center of the eyelids. This look is achieved by applying darker eyeshadow to the inner and outer corners of the eyes while placing a lighter, shimmery, or metallic shade in the middle. The result is a radiant, eye-catching glow that makes the eyes appear bigger and more dimensional. Halo eyes can be done with neutral tones for a soft, ethereal effect or with bold colors and glitter for a dramatic, high-impact look. It's a perfect choice for those who love a glamorous and luminous finish."

Cat Eyes

"Cat eyes is a classic and alluring eye makeup style that features a sharp, elongated wing to create a lifted and feline-like effect. This look is typically achieved using eyeliner, extending from the outer corner of the eyes to form a sleek, winged shape. Depending on the intensity, cat eyes can range from a subtle flick for everyday elegance to a bold, dramatic wing for a striking, sultry appearance. Often paired with smokey eyeshadow or a clean, precise line, this timeless technique enhances the eyes' shape and adds a touch of sophistication and glamour."

Fox eyes create a subtly fierce and sophisticated appearance, making the eyes look more lifted and elongated without harsh lines. This modern and elegant trend is perfect for achieving a snatched, sculpted look."

Graphic Eyeliner

"Graphic eyeliner is a bold and artistic eye makeup style that goes beyond traditional liner techniques to create unique, statement-making designs. It involves sharp lines, geometric shapes, negative space, and unconventional placements of eyeliner to add a modern, edgy touch to any look. This trend allows for endless creativity, from floating creases and double wings to abstract patterns and vibrant colors. Whether minimalistic or dramatic, graphic eyeliner is a striking way to express individuality and elevate eye makeup into a true work of art."

Soft eyeliner, often smudged or blended into the lashes, and fluttery lashes finish off the look, giving the eyes a soft, sultry appeal.
